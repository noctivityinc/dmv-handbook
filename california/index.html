About the California DMV Written Test

Number of questions on the test
46 Questions
Number of correct answers needed to pass
38 (83%)
Test failure rate
55%
Minimum age to take the test
15½ –17½ years old with either a Certificate of Completion of Driver Education or a provisional license
Number of times per application fee you may take the knowledge test
3 times (must wait 7 days between tries)
How is the test administered
At the DMV on a computer using a touchscreen application.
